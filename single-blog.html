SEVENTEEN Announces New Comeback Album and Global Promotions

  • SEVENTEEN News
  • 37 Comments

SEVENTEEN officially confirmed their upcoming comeback, exciting CARATs worldwide. The group teased new music, concept photos, and global promotions.

The comeback will showcase SEVENTEEN's signature teamwork and self-producing identity. Members actively participate in songwriting, choreography, and performance planning.

"We prepared this comeback to show our growth and gratitude to CARATs who always support us."

The album will feature multiple tracks highlighting each unit including Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit. Fans can also expect special stages and variety appearances.

SEVENTEEN plans to promote globally through concerts, fan meetings, and exclusive content releases.
