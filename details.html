SEVENTEEN Comeback Era & Global Activities

Every comeback era shows a new musical concept and storytelling style. From bright youth themes to mature emotional performances, SEVENTEEN constantly explores new artistic directions.

SEVENTEEN continues to grow as one of the most influential K-Pop groups in the world. Known as a self-producing idol group, SEVENTEEN actively participates in songwriting, choreography, and production.

The group also connects strongly with CARATs through fan meetings, world tours, online concerts, and interactive fan projects. Their teamwork and strong friendship are one of their biggest strengths.

SEVENTEEN Achievement & highlights

SEVENTEEN has received numerous awards from Korean and international music ceremonies.

Their world tours successfully sold out arenas across Asia, America, and Europe, proving their global popularity.

With 13 members divided into Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance units, SEVENTEEN delivers balanced musical and stage performances.

The group consistently dominates global music charts, album sales, and streaming platforms, proving their strong international influence.

SEVENTEEN continues to make waves in the K-Pop industry with their highly anticipated comebacks and musical innovations.
