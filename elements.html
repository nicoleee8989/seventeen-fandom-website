SEVENTEEN Introduction

SEVENTEEN is a South Korea boy group formed by PLEDIS Entertainment. The group debuted in 2015 and is known for their self-producing concept, where members actively participate in songwriting, choreography, and music production.

The group consists of 13 talented members divided into three units: Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit. Their teamwork and creativity have made them one of the most successful K-pop groups worldwide, gaining millions of fans known as CARATs.

SEVENTEEN Achievements

Since their debut, SEVENTEEN has released numerous hit albums and songs that have topped charts globally. Their synchronized choreography and energetic stage performances are widely praised.

The group has also held world tours, won multiple music awards, and built a strong bond with their fans through concerts, variety shows, and social media platforms.

SEVENTEEN units

Hip-Hop Unit

The Hip-Hop Unit focuses on rap and songwriting. Members include S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon. They contribute to powerful rap verses and lyrics in many SEVENTEEN songs.

Vocal Unit

The Vocal Unit highlights emotional vocals and harmonization. Members include Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, and Seungkwan. They create strong melodies and beautiful vocal arrangements.

Performance Unit

The Performance Unit focuses on choreography and stage creativity. Members include Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino. Their dance skills bring SEVENTEEN performances to life.
